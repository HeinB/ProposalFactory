# APPSolve Company History

APPSolve was established in 2002 as an IT Technology Services and Support provider, focusing on consulting services, support, and development.

The company was founded by Hein Blignaut, who developed APPSolve from an Oracle Applications DBA-focused company into an end-to-end technology services provider.

Over more than 23 years, APPSolve has acquired some of the best IT Technology skills in South Africa, growing to employ more than 50 Senior Consultants, each with 10 or more years' experience in the Enterprise Technology sphere.

## Geographic Expansion

APPSolve initially operated from offices in Gauteng and the Western Cape. Over subsequent years, the company extended operations into the sub-Saharan African market, with active and recent client engagements in Botswana, Zambia, Mozambique, Namibia, and Tanzania. APPSolve has also delivered projects in the USA, France, Abu Dhabi, and Pakistan.

## Awards and Recognition

APPSolve has been recognised by Oracle for innovation and partnership excellence:

- **Oracle Innovation Sustainability Award** — 2015 and 2016 (global award, for applications developed in Mozambique)
- **Oracle SaaS Partner of the Year (SADC Region)** — 2016
- **Oracle SaaS Partner of the Year (SADC Region) — New Entrant** — 2019
- **Oracle Business Impact Award — EMEA** — 2024
- **Oracle Business Impact Award — ECEMEA Region** — 2024

## Oracle Partnership

APPSolve has been an Oracle Partner for over two decades. The company holds Oracle Level 1 Partner status with published expertise in:

- Oracle Fusion Cloud Financials
- Oracle Fusion Cloud HCM Core
- Oracle Integration
- Oracle E-Business Suite Migration to OCI
- Oracle Cloud Infrastructure Migration

APPSolve is also:

- An authorised Oracle Value-Added Reseller — licensed to sell and distribute Oracle products
- A certified Oracle Cloud Excellence Implementer
- Specialised on Oracle Enterprise Linux

APPSolve's Oracle Level 1 Partner status entitles the company to sell and distribute Oracle licenses, provide clients with access to real-time global Oracle support, and implement solutions sustained by Oracle's global knowledge base.
