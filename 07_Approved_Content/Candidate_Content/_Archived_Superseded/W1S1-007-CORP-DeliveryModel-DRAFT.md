# APPSolve Delivery Model

APPSolve delivers technology services through several complementary service lines and a distinctive delivery philosophy.

## Products and Services

APPSolve supplies, implements, and supports the following products and services:

**Professional Advisory Services**
APPSolve has several experienced business consultants. We offer Project Management, System Financial Management, and Business Process Improvement Services across all industries and system implementations.

**Projects**
APPSolve scopes, architects, designs, configures, implements, and optimises a broad and integrated range of products within the Oracle enterprise application software stack, including Java Enterprise Edition.

**Customer Development**
APPSolve has built a number of custom applications recognised both locally and internationally. Areas of business requiring more specialised functionality than standard ERP systems can offer include Host-to-Host banking, stock and warehouse receipt systems, fuel management, and agricultural management systems.

**Managed Services**
APPSolve offers long-term support through a specialised managed services model. APPSolve supports and manages applications and technology on behalf of clients — both on-site and remotely — with niche skills in functional, technical, and DBA support.

**Business Development**
APPSolve is an authorised value-added reseller of Oracle products and services. The Gold Level partnership with Oracle **[UPDATE: verify current tier]** entitles APPSolve to sell and distribute Oracle licenses and provide access to real-time global Oracle support.

**Oracle Functional Services**
APPSolve's experienced Oracle Applications Functional Consultants design overall solutions and business processes, and configure Oracle Applications modules. Areas of expertise include Finance, Projects, HRMS, Payroll, and Supply Chain.

**Technical Services (Oracle and Custom)**
APPSolve's Applications Technical Developers have skills including XML Publisher, OBIEE, ADF, OAF, SOA, Workflow, BPEL, and BPM. The team develops end-to-end custom processes to complement ERP systems. APPSolve has over **[UPDATE: 24+]** years of system integration expertise.

**Oracle Applications DBA Services**
APPSolve has the largest number of locally based Oracle Applications DBAs in South Africa. Services include:
- Database monitoring and uptime management
- Trace file and datapump export monitoring
- Datafile backup monitoring
- Performance monitoring and tuning
- Tablespace management and change management
- Linux OS and Oracle Virtual Machine support

**System Administration, Virtualisation, and Engineered Systems**
APPSolve provides specialist services in virtualisation, operating system, and Oracle Engineered System space — including Oracle Database Appliances, Oracle Enterprise Linux, and Oracle Virtual Machines.

## How APPSolve Delivers

APPSolve's delivery philosophy rests on three principles:

1. **Senior resources only** — all consultants have 10+ years' experience; no junior resources placed on client environments
2. **Flexible billing** — Time and Materials, Fixed Price, or APPSolve's unique Monthly Recurring Invoice Model
3. **Trusted partner approach** — APPSolve works alongside clients rather than as a transactional supplier

### Monthly Recurring Invoice Model

APPSolve's Monthly Recurring Invoice Model provides a flexible and predictable costing method. The service is measured on time spent but monthly costs remain stable. This model ensures it is in the best interest of both parties to continuously work towards a stable and optimised environment.
