# APPSolve Company Overview

APPSolve is a software technology support company that specialises in providing cutting-edge support techniques, resources, and models to deliver competitive offers that are difficult for competitors to match.

APPSolve was established in 2002 as an IT Technology Services and Support provider, focusing on consulting services, support, and development. Over more than 23 years, APPSolve has acquired some of the best IT Technology skills in South Africa. We operate from offices in Gauteng and the Western Cape.

APPSolve employs more than 50 Senior Consultants, each with 10 or more years' experience in the Enterprise Technology sphere. We have been especially valuable as a solution partner in the Public Sector, Telco, Agriculture, Financial Services, and Higher Education industries.

## Our Approach

APPSolve's unique business model has a strong "trusted partner" focus, offering experienced business and IT resources to clients. This has been one of the main reasons for the company's continued growth. Our track record of satisfied customers and a team of motivated and highly skilled employees is our proud legacy.

One of APPSolve's core strengths is understanding our clients' expectations. We ensure that our clients receive the most value from their IT investment. Our proactive approach of making software function optimally has saved our clients a considerable amount of money, thus enabling growth. Our reputation is built on delivering on-time, within budget, and exceeding client expectations.

## International Footprint

APPSolve has active and recent client engagements across the sub-Saharan African market, including Botswana, Zambia, Mozambique, Namibia, and Tanzania. APPSolve has also delivered projects in the USA, France, Abu Dhabi, and Pakistan.
