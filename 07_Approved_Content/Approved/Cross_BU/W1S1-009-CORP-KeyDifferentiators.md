# APPSolve Key Differentiators

## Why Work with APPSolve?

APPSolve's delivery model is built around seven core differentiators:

### 1. Least Risk Option

APPSolve offers the least risk with regards to technology service requirements because:

- Deep experience deploying from both an application and industry perspective
- Proposed teams consist of experienced resources with cross-domain expertise
- Familiarity with enterprise Oracle and Acumatica environments and their unique intricacies

### 2. Technical Expertise and Track Record

APPSolve has a team of leaders in ERP Systems and Integration. Our track record demonstrates successful management of complex sites and large environments — technically, functionally, and relationally. Our subject matter experts ensure practical solutions deployed without lengthy discovery periods.

### 3. Service Delivery Management Team

APPSolve's experienced Service Delivery Managers handle the environment, resources, projects, and client relationships as a team. This team approach ensures no interruptions to service delivery. The team is always available.

### 4. On-site Expertise Support

APPSolve's on-site team is backed by a pool of over 50 senior resources with more than 10 years' experience each. These resources are available should the need arise, providing depth beyond the primary team.

### 5. Continuous Improvement Model

APPSolve continuously innovates and strives to use the latest technology to scale environments, drive consolidation, and automate. The Continuous Improvement Model consists of four stages — **Analyse, Plan, Implement, Measure** — applied across three pillars: **People, Processes, and Technology**.

This model ensures that operational effort decreases over time, freeing capacity for strategic value delivery.

### 6. Hybrid Support Model

APPSolve's Hybrid Support Model is distinctive in the Southern African market. It provides a cost-effective way to manage a site through a blended model of on-site and remote support personnel.

Key benefits:
- More affordable rates through rate-blending
- Easier risk management
- Flexibility around resource and time management
- Remote Support Centre providing 24x7 monitoring capability with after-hours support services
- Same level of service at reduced cost

### 7. Flexible and Predictable Costing

APPSolve offers three costing models:

| Model | Description |
|---|---|
| Time and Materials | Standard billing by time |
| Fixed Price | Agreed scope at fixed cost |
| Monthly Recurring Invoice | APPSolve's unique model — predictable monthly cost, flexible capacity |

The **Monthly Recurring Invoice Model** is APPSolve's most distinctive offering: a fixed monthly cost that provides flexibility to transfer capacity between months. Both parties are incentivised to maintain a stable, optimised environment.
