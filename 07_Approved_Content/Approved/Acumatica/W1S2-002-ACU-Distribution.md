# Acumatica Distribution Edition

## Overview

Acumatica Distribution Edition is a comprehensive, cloud-based ERP solution designed for distributors. It helps manage various aspects of distribution operations, including inventory management, order processing, purchasing, and customer relationships. Acumatica's flexible platform is designed to scale with growing businesses and can be customised to meet specific distribution industry needs.

## Benefits

- **Scalability:** Designed for growing distributors, Acumatica can scale to handle increasing volumes of transactions and data as the business expands.
- **Real-Time Visibility:** With a unified platform, businesses gain real-time visibility into inventory, order statuses, and financial data, helping to make data-driven decisions.
- **Automation:** Streamlines repetitive tasks such as order processing, inventory updates, and purchasing, reducing manual intervention and errors.
- **Improved Customer Satisfaction:** By integrating CRM and order management, Acumatica helps ensure that customer orders are fulfilled accurately and on time.
- **Cloud-Based Access:** As a cloud-based solution, Acumatica is accessible from anywhere, enabling remote management of distribution operations and supporting a mobile workforce.
- **Customisable:** The platform can be customised to fit specific business processes and industry requirements, making it versatile across different distribution verticals.

## Key Features and Capabilities

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| Order Management | Streamlines order-to-cash processes, managing sales orders, pricing, and fulfilment. Supports advanced order types including sales, drop shipments, and backorders. Real-time integration with inventory, shipping, and finance. Automated workflows for approvals, credit checking, and customer communications. |
| Procurement and Purchase Order Management | Enables automated purchasing processes including requisition approvals, vendor selection, and order placement. Integrates with inventory to maintain optimal stock levels. Vendor relationship management including purchase histories and contract management. Real-time updates on order status, vendor lead times, and delivery tracking. |
| Sales and Customer Management | Full CRM functionality to manage customer relationships, sales pipelines, and service requests. 360-degree visibility into customer interactions, sales orders, and order history. Supports automated marketing campaigns and lead management. Real-time integration with sales orders for accurate customer service and sales forecasting. |
| Financial Management Integration | Fully integrated with Acumatica's financial management suite for real-time updates on accounts payable, accounts receivable, and general ledger. Automated billing processes aligned with order fulfilment. Comprehensive reporting and analytics for financial performance including margin analysis and cost control. |
