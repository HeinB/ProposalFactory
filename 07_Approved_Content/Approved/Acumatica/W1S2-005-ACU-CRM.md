# Acumatica CRM (Customer Relationship Management)

## Overview

Customer Relationship Management (CRM) is not an add-on in Acumatica — it is fully integrated with the other suites. The Customer Management Suite uses dashboards and reports to provide real-time customer information on all customer activities: quotes, orders, invoices, payments, and support cases or service calls. A self-service Customer Portal lets you give customers access to the information they need.

## Benefits

- Maintains a real-time view of customer activities across all operations
- Integrates fully with sales, inventory, purchasing, accounting, and financial reporting
- Reduces response times and improves customer satisfaction
- Enables complete sales pipeline visibility and forecasting
- Case management and service request tracking built into the same platform

## Key Features

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| Contact and Account Management | Centralised repository for all customer, prospect, and partner information with full activity history. |
| Sales Pipeline Management | Lead and opportunity tracking with probability forecasting and pipeline visibility. |
| Quote Management | Create and manage quotes directly linked to inventory and pricing rules. Conversion to sales orders in one step. |
| Case Management | Customer support case tracking, escalation workflows, and resolution tracking — integrated with the service module. |
| Customer Portal | Self-service portal allowing customers to view orders, invoices, and submit cases or service requests. |
| Marketing Activities | Campaign management, lead import, and activity tracking to support marketing-to-sales workflows. |
| Outlook Integration | Synchronise emails, calendar items, and contacts between Microsoft Outlook and Acumatica. |
| Dashboards and Reporting | Role-based CRM dashboards with real-time pipeline data, activity metrics, and customer performance reports. |
