# Oracle Fusion Cloud Capability Statement

## Section 1 — Oracle Fusion Platform Overview

Oracle Fusion is a suite of enterprise business applications designed to streamline and modernise enterprise processes. It integrates financial management, human capital management, supply chain, procurement, customer relationship management, and governance functions into a unified, cloud-native platform.

**Key platform characteristics:**

| Characteristic | Description |
|---|---|
| Modular Architecture | Organisations implement individual applications or the full suite, scaling as requirements evolve |
| Cloud-Native (SaaS) | Platform delivered over the internet, eliminating on-premises hardware requirements |
| Integrated Suite | Works seamlessly across all Fusion modules and with existing Oracle investments (EBS, PeopleSoft, JD Edwards) |
| Advanced Analytics | Embedded business intelligence and real-time analytics across all modules |
| User Experience | Role-based dashboards, personalised views, and mobile access for all devices |
| Global Capabilities | Multi-currency, multi-language, and multi-GAAP support for multinational operations |
| Security and Compliance | Built-in security controls, data encryption, and regulatory compliance tooling |

*Source: TMPL-001 lines 112–141; PayInc HCM lines 80–89 — identical text across both proposals confirms stable platform description*

**Oracle Fusion core component overview:**

| Module | Scope |
|---|---|
| Oracle Fusion Financials | General ledger, accounts payable, accounts receivable, fixed assets, cash management |
| Oracle Fusion Human Capital Management (HCM) | Payroll interface, talent management, workforce management, HR analytics |
| Oracle Fusion Supply Chain Management (SCM) | Procurement, inventory management, order management, product lifecycle management |
| Oracle Fusion Customer Relationship Management (CRM) | Sales automation, marketing, service, social media engagement |
| Oracle Fusion Governance, Risk and Compliance (GRC) | Risk management, policy enforcement, regulatory compliance |

*Source: TMPL-001 lines 124–141; PayInc HCM lines 91–107*

## Section 3 — Oracle Fusion HCM

APPSolve is a specialist Oracle Fusion HCM implementer with multi-module, multi-sector, and multi-country delivery experience.

### 3.1 Global Human Resources (Core HR)

Oracle Fusion Global HR centralises employee data and provides lifecycle management tools covering hiring, onboarding, job changes, transfers, and terminations. The module supports:

- Single employee master record spanning personal, position, compensation, and history data
- Real-time visual organisation charts with position management and reporting lines
- Configurable workflow approvals aligned to supervisor hierarchy
- Multi-country, multi-currency, multi-legislation employee management

**Benefits:**
- A single source of truth for all workforce data
- Elimination of duplicate administrative effort and data inconsistencies
- Foundation for all dependent HCM modules

*Source: SAA HCM RFP Section 2 (Global HR module); PayInc HCM lines 127–130, 160–164*

### 3.2 AI-Based Dynamic Skills

Oracle Fusion AI Skills enables organisations to build and maintain a dynamic skills inventory across the workforce. The module uses AI to recommend skill development paths and align workforce capability to business objectives.

**Key features:**
- AI-driven skills profiling and gap identification
- Integration with Talent Management, Learning Cloud, and Recruiting for end-to-end workforce planning
- Dynamic skills libraries aligned to current and future role requirements

*Source: SAA HCM RFP (AI Skills module); PayInc HCM lines 130–134*

### 3.3 Absence Management

Oracle Fusion Absence Management provides a comprehensive solution for managing all types of employee leave and absence, including:

- Configurable absence plans aligned to company policy and legislative requirements
- Employee self-service leave requests with automated approval workflows
- Integration with time management, payroll, and workforce planning for seamless absence processing
- Absence liability reporting and analytics

*Source: SAA HCM RFP (Absence Management module); PayInc HCM lines 135–139*

### 3.4 Benefits Administration

Oracle Fusion Benefits supports the full benefits lifecycle from plan configuration through enrolment to administration. The module enables:

- Flexible plan design across medical, retirement, insurance, and voluntary benefits
- Self-service enrolment for employees with guided eligibility rules
- Integration with payroll for automated deduction processing
- Regulatory compliance and year-end reporting

*Source: SAA HCM RFP (Benefits module); PayInc HCM lines 140–144*

### 3.5 Oracle Journeys

Oracle Journeys provides guided, personalised digital workflows for employee lifecycle events — onboarding, offboarding, role changes, and wellbeing programmes. The module:

- Assembles task lists, content, and actions across HR, IT, Facilities, and Finance
- Delivers guided experiences via desktop and mobile
- Supports custom journeys aligned to company-specific events

*Source: SAA HCM RFP (Journeys module); PayInc HCM lines 145–149*

### 3.6 Payroll Interface

Oracle Fusion Payroll Interface provides the certified integration layer between Oracle HCM and third-party payroll systems. APPSolve has extensive experience delivering this integration across multiple clients.

- Other third-party systems via OIC

## Section 5 — Oracle Fusion Procurement

Oracle Fusion Procurement is a comprehensive suite of cloud-based applications designed to streamline and enhance the procurement process. It integrates procurement activities — from supplier sourcing through contract management, purchase order execution, and invoice processing — enabling organisations to manage procurement efficiently with full policy compliance and spend visibility.

**Oracle Fusion Procurement benefits:**
- Cost Reduction: Centralising and streamlining procurement processes reduces costs and eliminates inefficiencies
- Improved Supplier Relationships: Enhanced collaboration tools foster better relationships with suppliers
- Increased Visibility: Real-time insights and analytics provide visibility into all procurement activities
- Compliance and Risk Management: Built-in contract and policy compliance reduces non-compliance and procurement risk

### 5.1 Core Procurement Capabilities

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| Procurement Contracts | Manages the entire lifecycle of procurement contracts — creation, negotiation, approval, and management. Ensures compliance with organisational policies and legal requirements |
| Self-Service Procurement | Allows employees to request goods and services through a user-friendly, catalog-based interface. Reduces maverick spending by guiding employees towards approved suppliers and negotiated pricing |
| Sourcing | Facilitates strategic sourcing — supplier identification, bid collection, and bid evaluation. Supports RFQs (Request for Quotation) and reverse auctions. Enables collaboration with suppliers and internal stakeholders |
| Supplier Qualification Management | Assesses and manages supplier risk and performance. Automates the qualification process, ensuring only qualified suppliers participate in procurement activities |
| Supplier Portal | Platform for suppliers to manage profiles, view purchase orders, submit invoices, and track payments — reducing invoice processing friction for both parties |
| Spend Analysis | Analyses procurement spending to identify cost-saving opportunities. Provides insights into supplier performance, spend categories, and trends |
| Purchase Order Management | Manages creation, approval, and tracking of purchase orders. Ensures that all purchases align with contracts and organisational policies |

*Source: BankServAfrica ERP Proposal (August 2024) lines 561–604*

### 5.2 Self-Service Procurement

Oracle Fusion Self-Service Procurement provides a consumer-like purchasing experience that empowers employees to manage their own purchasing needs while maintaining full policy compliance:

- **Consumer-like interface:** Employees search, compare, and order goods from curated catalogs of approved suppliers and products
- **Guided buying:** System routes users towards preferred suppliers and negotiated pricing, reducing off-contract spend
- **Automated approval workflows:** Requisitions automatically route for approval based on configured business rules — spending limits, departmental approvals, project-based controls
- **Real-time budget checks:** Integrates with Oracle Financials to validate budget availability before purchase commitment, preventing overspend
- **Self-service receiving:** Users confirm receipt directly in the system, automatically updating inventory and procurement records
- **Spend reporting:** Tools for managers to analyse spend patterns, supplier performance, and policy compliance

*Source: BankServAfrica ERP Proposal lines 717–805*
