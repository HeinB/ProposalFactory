## Gap Detail

### GAP-001 — B-BBEE Certificate ⚠ CRITICAL

- **Asset:** COMP-001
- **Expiry:** 2026-07-31 (46 days)
- **Risk:** Any tender submitted after 2026-07-31 without a valid B-BBEE certificate will be non-responsive on preferential procurement criteria. This affects all public sector tenders and most enterprise tenders.
- **Impact:** Loss of 20 B-BBEE points on 90/10 scoring = near-certain loss on competitive public sector tenders.
- **Mitigation:** Engage BEE rating agency immediately. Confirm whether current Level 3 audit can be converted to Level 2 before expiry (prior cert RS-17968-0724-C58 achieved Level 2 — review what changed). Target Level 2 for maximum B-BBEE preferential points.
- **Owner:** Finance Director
- **Deadline:** Certificate must be issued before 2026-07-31

---

### GAP-002 — Directors' Resolution ⚠ CRITICAL

- **Asset:** COMP-011
- **Expired:** 2026-03-01 (3+ months overdue)
- **Risk:** Submitting tenders with an expired resolution creates a legal validity question for any contract executed. Some evaluators reject submissions where the authority document is > 12 months old.
- **Impact:** Potential rejection at compliance evaluation stage.
- **Mitigation:** Draft updated resolution (template available in `Tender Pack/Resolution/`); obtain board/director signatures; date 2026-06-15 or later.
- **Owner:** Company Secretary
- **Deadline:** Before any further tender submission

---

### GAP-003 — Public Liability Insurance ✗ HIGH

- **Asset:** COMP-008
- **Status:** Missing — no certificate found in any directory
- **Risk:** Many enterprise and government tenders require both Professional Indemnity and Public Liability. Inability to produce PL certificate = disqualification from those tenders.
- **Context:** Professional Indemnity is confirmed active (COMP-007). PL is typically a separate line item on the same policy schedule. Possible that PL exists but the certificate has not been downloaded and filed.
- **Mitigation:** Contact African Eagle (broker path in `Tender Pack/Insurance/insurance details.txt`). Request PL confirmation certificate. If not currently held, obtain cover alongside PI renewal (Nov 2026).
- **Owner:** Finance Director
- **Deadline:** Within 14 days — contact broker to confirm

---

### GAP-004 — POPIA Policy / Data Protection Manual ✗ HIGH

- **Asset:** COMP-012
- **Status:** Missing — only a client consent form found (not a company policy)
- **Risk:** POPIA has been in effect since July 2021. Financial services buyers (PSGFS, Investec, KPMG-type) increasingly require vendors to demonstrate POPIA compliance. Without a documented policy, APPSolve cannot credibly answer POPIA questionnaire items in tenders.
- **Evidence of risk:** PSGFS RFP (June 2026) included data protection questions — answered with general POPIA compliance statements. Without a formal policy document, these answers cannot be substantiated.
- **Mitigation:** Draft a POPIA Policy document covering: lawful basis for processing, data subject rights, breach notification, Information Officer appointment, sub-processor agreements. Engage legal counsel for a one-off review. Register Information Officer with Information Regulator.
- **Owner:** Company Secretary / Designated Information Officer
- **Deadline:** Within 60 days

---

### GAP-005 — Acumatica Company-Level Partner Certificate ✗ HIGH

- **Asset:** COMP-016
- **Status:** Missing — only individual consultant certifications found
- **Risk:** Tenders requiring proof of Acumatica Gold Partner / authorised VAR status expect a company-level certificate. Individual certifications do not satisfy this requirement.
- **Note:** APPSolve is confirmed as an Acumatica Gold Partner in KB asset W1S1-004-ACU-AcumaticaPartnership.md. The certificate exists on the Acumatica Partner Portal but has not been downloaded and filed.
- **Mitigation:** Log into `partner.acumatica.com`, navigate to Partner Certificates, download current Gold Partner company certificate as PDF. File in `Tender Pack/Certifications - Oracle and Other/Acumatica/`.
- **Owner:** Acumatica BU Lead
- **Deadline:** Within 7 days (quick fix)

### GAP-008 — PAIA Manual ✗ MEDIUM

- **Asset:** COMP-013
- **Status:** Missing
- **Legal obligation:** Section 51 of PAIA requires all private bodies with 50+ employees to prepare and make available an access manual. If APPSolve has 50+ employees, this is a legal requirement, not just a tender requirement.
- **Risk:** Required by public sector tenders; absence visible to evaluators.
- **Mitigation:** Draft Section 51 manual using SAHRC template. Register with SAHRC. File in `01_Compliance/`. Low cost — template-based task.
- **Owner:** Company Secretary
- **Deadline:** Within 60 days
