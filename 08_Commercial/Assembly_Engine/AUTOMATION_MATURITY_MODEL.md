# Automation Maturity Model

**Date:** 2026-06-25 | **Version:** 1.0 | **Status:** Approved  
**Work Package:** WP18A — Proposal Factory Architecture

---

## 1. Maturity Level Definitions

| Level | Name | Description |
|---|---|---|
| **L1** | Manual | Section is authored from scratch by a human for every tender. No reusable templates or KB source. Output quality depends entirely on the individual author. |
| **L2** | Template-Driven | A template exists that guides authoring. Human still writes all content but uses a structure. KB source exists but is not automatically retrieved or assembled. |
| **L3** | Deterministic Assembly | Content is selected and assembled from approved KB assets by rule, without AI judgement. Human does not write content — they validate it. Output is reproducible. This is the target for all content sourced from the Capability Library, Assumption Library, or Reference Library. |
| **L4** | AI-Assisted Generation | Content is generated by AI using approved KB sources as input. AI drafts the text; human reviews and approves before use. Applied where content must be tailored to the specific tender but a KB source exists as a foundation. |
| **L5** | Fully Automated | Content is assembled, validated, and formatted without human authoring or review, other than final BU Lead governance sign-off. Only sections with purely deterministic content, no commercial sensitivity, and complete KB coverage reach L5. |
