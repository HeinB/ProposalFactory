# Acumatica Inventory Control

## Overview

Acumatica Inventory Control is a comprehensive module within the Acumatica Cloud ERP platform that helps businesses manage and track their inventory across multiple warehouses, locations, and business processes. It provides real-time visibility into stock levels, automates replenishment processes, and integrates with other business functions like purchasing, sales, and manufacturing to streamline operations.

## Benefits

- **Improved Efficiency:** Automation reduces manual processes and errors, leading to improved operational efficiency.
- **Cost Savings:** Helps reduce excess inventory and stockouts, improving cash flow and lowering carrying costs.
- **Enhanced Accuracy:** Real-time tracking and reporting ensure inventory data is always up to date and accurate.
- **Scalability:** Acumatica is built to scale with growing businesses, offering flexibility for managing larger inventories across multiple locations.
- **Real-Time Decision-Making:** Real-time visibility into stock levels and inventory performance enables better and faster decision-making.

## Key Features and Capabilities

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| Real-Time Inventory Visibility | Centralised inventory management providing a unified view across multiple warehouses, locations, and companies. Real-time stock level tracking (on-hand, available, allocated, in-transit). Multi-location tracking across separate warehouses, retail stores, or divisions. |
| Inventory Transactions | Inventory adjustments for shrinkage, theft, or errors with audit trail. Transfers between warehouses or locations with complete tracking. Automated recording of receipts from vendors and shipments to customers. Kitting and assembly for products composed of multiple inventory items. |
| Lot and Serial Number Tracking | Lot/batch tracking for traceability through the supply chain. Serial number tracking for individual high-value or regulated products. Expiration date management for perishable or time-sensitive products. |
| Replenishment and Stock Management | Automated purchase orders based on predefined reorder points, minimum/maximum stock levels, or lead times. Safety stock and reorder point management. |
| Multiple Costing Methods | Supports FIFO, Weighted Average Cost, and Standard Costing in compliance with IFRS/IAS 2. Accurate and detailed inventory valuation in financial reports. |
| Inventory Allocation and Reservations | Automatic reservation for sales orders, production orders, or shipments. Backorders management with fulfilment prioritisation based on availability and order priority. |
| Inventory Reporting and Analytics | Inventory valuation reports, turnover and aging reports, stock availability reports across locations. Customisable dashboards displaying inventory turns, reorder points, and stock levels. |

**Remediation notes (2026-06-10 — Session B Part 1):**
- **Multiple Costing Methods row:** LIFO removed. LIFO (Last-In, First-Out) inventory costing is prohibited under IFRS/IAS 2, which governs inventory accounting in South Africa. Original read: *"Supports FIFO, LIFO, Weighted Average Cost, and Standard Costing."* Corrected to: *"Supports FIFO, Weighted Average Cost, and Standard Costing in compliance with IFRS/IAS 2."* The remaining three costing methods are valid under IAS 2.
- **Replenishment and Stock Management row:** Removed "Demand forecasting integration." Acumatica does not include a native demand forecasting module — this capability requires third-party integration. Citing it as a built-in feature without qualification would be inaccurate in an SA tender context.
