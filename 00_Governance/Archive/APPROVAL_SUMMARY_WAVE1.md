### Key Factual Corrections Applied

Six factual errors or outdated claims were corrected across the Session A files before approval:

1. **Headcount** — Templates stated "110+" and "over 100 Senior Consultants". Corrected to confirmed "50+ Senior Consultants" (F2). Earlier figures were likely inflated across multiple template generations.

2. **Oracle partner tier** — September 2024 template cited "Gold Level partnership" and "Gold Certified" three years after Oracle Gold Membership expired (August 2021, per EXPIRED_APPSolve_OPN_202108.pdf). Corrected to Oracle Level 1 Partner with five published expertise areas (F3/F4).

3. **Geography** — Templates cited Nigeria, Uganda, Bangladesh, Qatar, and Ghana as markets. No client folder evidence was found for any of these across 18,400 files. All five removed. Sub-Saharan coverage corrected to Botswana, Zambia, Mozambique, Namibia, and Tanzania (all confirmed by multiple client folders).

4. **SAP BeBanking** — AI extractor had added "SAP integration capability" to W1S1-005. Zero BeBanking proposals referencing SAP were found across the full corpus; the SAP reference was traced to a Columbus Consulting JV agreement. Claim removed entirely (F10).

5. **Oracle awards** — The EMEA/ECEMEA Business Impact Award entry was undated in the September 2024 template. Confirmed as 2024 (EMEA and ECEMEA) by Hein Blignaut (F7) and corroborated by absence from the 2023 Columbus Consulting company profile.

6. **Founder biographical statement** — "a significant force in the South African Oracle industry" (W1S1-002): unverifiable promotional superlative removed in favour of factual descriptive language.

### Major Risks Removed

1. **Oracle Gold Partner claim** — Citing an expired Oracle Gold Membership in tender responses is a verifiable misrepresentation. Removed from all 9 Session A files.

2. **Geography inflation** — Active operations claimed in Nigeria, Uganda, Bangladesh, and Qatar without any supporting client evidence. If an evaluator had required references for any of these markets, the claim could not have been substantiated. All four removed.

3. **Headcount overstatement** — Claiming 110+ or 100+ consultants when the confirmed figure is 50+. Overstating company size in a tender response is a credibility risk if challenged. Corrected uniformly across all affected files.

4. **Unconfirmed SAP integration** — A capability claim with zero source document evidence. If cited in a BeBanking tender, a technically sophisticated evaluator could have challenged it. Removed entirely.

5. **Promotional superlative in founder bio** — "a significant force in the South African Oracle industry" (W1S1-002): unverifiable self-assessment removed. The factual description stands without it.
