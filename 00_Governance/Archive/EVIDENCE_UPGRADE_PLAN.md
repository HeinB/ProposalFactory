# APPSolve Tender Knowledge Base — Evidence Upgrade Plan

**Version:** 1.0 | **Created:** 2026-06-14 | **Owner:** Hein Blignaut (BU Lead)
**Purpose:** Map every approved capability statement to its current evidence tier, identify gaps, and define the upgrade path to Tier 1 evidence or record a formal governance exception.

> **Governance rule:** Every approved capability statement must have Tier 1 evidence OR a formal governance exception recorded in this document. Tier 2 is not a permanent acceptable state — it is an interim status pending evidence upgrade or exception registration.

> **Evidence Tier Definitions:**
> - **Tier 1 — Confirmed Delivery:** Won engagement; go-live confirmed; implementation delivered.
> - **Tier 2 — Active Implementation or Platform Capability:** Active pipeline, or platform-level capability confirmed by product documentation and APPSolve's technical expertise, but no independently referenceable completed delivery.
> - **Tier 3 — PoC or Proposed Only:** Proof of concept or proposal only — no confirmed delivery.
> - **Internal Evidence:** Confirmed delivery but client cannot be named (Rule 21.4 / similar governance restriction).
> - **Active Pipeline:** Delivery in progress — not yet referenceable (Rule 21.5).
